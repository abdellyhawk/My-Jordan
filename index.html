Jordan's world Map

Jordan is a country in the Middle East, located at the crossroads of Asia, Africa, and Europe. It shares borders with Saudi Arabia to the south and east, Iraq to the northeast, Syria to the north, and Palestine to the west.

tourist attractions in Jordan

Jordan is a land of ancient history and stunning landscapes. It is home to the iconic city of Petra and the therapeutic waters of the Dead Sea. The vast deserts of Wadi Rum offer breathtaking scenery and adventure. With its rich culture and diverse attractions, Jordan promises an unforgettable experience.

Petra

Known as the "Rose-Red City," Petra is an ancient Nabatean city carved into rock over 2,000 years ago. Its iconic Treasury (Al-Khazneh) and Monastery (Ad-Deir) attract millions of visitors annually and make it a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Wadi Rum

Also called the "Valley of the Moon," this desert landscape features stunning sandstone mountains, natural rock arches, and vast red dunes. It's a popular spot for camping, stargazing, and adventure activities like jeep tours and camel rides.

Dead Sea

The lowest point on Earth, the Dead Sea is famous for its high salt content, allowing visitors to effortlessly float on its surface. Its mineral-rich waters and mud are known for their therapeutic properties.

Aqaba

Jordan’s port city on the Red Sea is a paradise for water sports enthusiasts. Aqaba offers beautiful beaches, coral reefs for snorkeling and diving, and luxury resorts.

Jerash

Home to some of the best-preserved Roman ruins outside of Italy, Jerash features stunning colonnaded streets, temples, theaters, and an ancient hippodrome, showcasing the grandeur of the Roman Empire..
